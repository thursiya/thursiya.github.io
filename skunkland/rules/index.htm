The Skunkland server was founded in December 2011. It is a vanilla survival server, so no mods, PvP, or creative builds; everything was made the good old-fashioned way, block-by-mined-block while avoiding creepers! The server is whitelisted to prevent griefing and theft. If you know someone who'd like to join the server please make sure they are at least 13 years old and get them to read through and understand these rules then contact the server admin, thursiya.

Rules

Thou Shalt Not Grief or Steal

We choose to play on a white-listed server for a reason: we want to build in peace and be able to return to our creations in the same state we left them. Simply put, don't modify another player's build or take items that are not yours or given to you. We have been fortunate not to have to worry about these problems on our server and hope to stay that way.

As part of our peaceful co-existance, our server also has no PvP. Players are unable to damage each other. Players may choose to build traps or dangerous locations but we ask that players do not falsely advertise safety in their builds. One way you can watch out for others is by properly lighting your build so visitors don't have to worry about mobs (see Lighting Basics).


Thou Shalt Replant

On our server we like to maintain the world in a fairly natural state. Admins remove world generation errors like floating blocks/islands and clean up trim edges as part of maintenance. As a player traveling around the greater server world you are expected to leave the world in the state you found it. That means replanting trees, sugar cane, and cacti that you harvest (and that you harvest fully). It also means repairing creeper damage you cause (or getting admin help if not possible).

Obviously the world is changed when players choose to build in an area, so these rules are meant for maintaining areas of the world beyond builds.

Conventions

Note: We are currently using a single player sleep algorithm that sets the world to day if only one player sleeps. The players can skip the night phase if all players logged into the server are in beds at the same time. To inform other players that you wish to skip the night phase send the chat 'zzz' when you are in bed. If another player has indicated that they wish to sleep you should find a bed to sleep in (and type 'zzz' to show you are in bed) or log out if a bed is not nearby.

Much of the world of Skunkland is connected by a network of subways. Inside each subway station is a chest (or dispenser) containing minecarts for visitors to use. If you need to borrow a minecart try to replace it later to keep the lending system working.

Notes on Abandoned Builds

Occasionally players choose to give up on their builds in Skunkland and (hopefully) inform the admin that their build sites are abandoned. You can find abandoned builds on the political map marked with a light grey outline or ask the server admin if a site is abandoned (few are).

Abandoned builds are open to other players to build in and claim provided that any build that was already completed at the site be maintained. This could mean building around it or adding to it as part of a revitalization project. Structures built for the express purpose of survival/playing the game/aquiring resources (such as survival shacks, strip mines, animal pens, vegetable gardens, tree farms, etc.) may be removed or altered. Usually you can identify a build to be maintained by the presence of signs or purely aesthetic markings. If it is at all unclear what must be maintained at an abandoned build then contact the admin. Assuming the new builder is willing to keep these things in mind then that player is free to build at the site, claim the land, and any goods left behind in chests, etc. Abandonded builds are not free game for looting.
