The province of Faclair Capital encompasses the original lands of Faclair, starting from the community of Cadwgan on Delve's border. Spreading southwest, Endermere was founded (then later absorbed into Cadwgan), and a grand capital was laid out in Auverland, Faclair's chief city.

Venturing to the northwest of the province, one finds the massive World Tree, sitting amid the artificial jungle, both testament to the enduring importance of trees and nature to the people of Faclair.
