*** First City TV News Network ***

Lunar Hill, Gavel: A small group of what appear to be protesters have gathered outside of the Operis Lunar Hill office. The group is similar to others that have occasionally sprung up across the country, avoiding vigilant big city security forces in favour of smaller communities. The latest concern is the shift to factory farming in Operis operations. Reached for comment, an Operis spokesperson responded, "The market has dictated a need for increased production. We continue to focus on meeting shareholder expectations." This wouldn't be the first commercial controversy here, as unfounded rumours persist of slave labour bolstering the economy.

World Map

The mapping software has finally been updated to handle the world changes brought in 1.13, and now a new political map is available to you! The map has expanded westward some to encompass new build areas, and the whole thing had to be redone from scratch in new software due to the unwieldy nature and massive file size of the vector-heavy old format. Main additions are a few shifted borders, new region names and updated settlement names, and the states of Lumina and Sedona.

Featured Build: The Banking System

The first bank in Skunkland was the Bank of Gavel New Kingsburg Regional Branch, founded back in the first century. Back then, the main function of banks was to offer access to the newly-added ender chests and storage for visiting players. Fast-forward to the present, and banking is now the foundation of the massive server economy. While still offering storage services, now players can deposit emeralds to buy one of the server's 3 digital currencies and use that money to buy goods and services around the world. Today there are 3 different bank franchises operating, including the Bank of the North and the Tseles Bank of Commerce (the New Kingsburg branch, the latest bank branch completed, is featured in the image). Banks can be found in every major city in Skunkland, and businesses offering items for sale are usually not too far off.
