Happy New Year from the heart of Skunkland! That's the year 236 around these parts. We are still happily running version 1.8.1 with few hiccups. Our website continues to be regularly updated with new information and pictures from Skunkland.

Most of the developments in the last couple of months have been happening in the south for a change. Not content with clearing an ocean monument and building a small guardian farm, Spaceskunks has been working on gold farms (harvesting zombie pigmen) in both the nether and Alaris province (Delve) - this after completing exterior construction of the fabulous castle Ulvenstand in Ulve (Ennist, Delve). Shylilie has been building out the last major spaces in Auverland including a hedge maze, park, and warehouse and has begun a large-scale project near the Jungle Tours in Faclair. Taking a break from hollowing out the streets of Celestia and the Gavel Underdeeps, Thursiya has completed exterior construction of the promised (and yet-to-be-named) tower in New Kingsburg and is awaiting the last Century Build participants' choice of real estate. All three aforementioned players teamed up to build subways to the Grand Mesa and found a mesa mining town, Red Rock. Meanwhile Quackey958 has begun construction of an above-ground light rail to connect Badrum's Imperial City with Alf-Wy. Subway routes have now been cut south from Delve and east from Cadwgan to Ulve, with a further route heading southeast to Red Rock. Subway routes now also close the loop between Spawn - Red Rock - Dragon Ridge - Sofia - Johnville, allowing faster access to the south east.

The latest Skunkland political map is here. At first glance it may not look particularly different from the last but pay close attention to the large expansions in Faclair and Delve, plus a significant southern expansion of the subway system. Also more of the trimlines from the last big trim have been cleaned up. Want to carve out your own piece of the map? Contact thursiya about designating your space as a national build or if you have any local names and tidbits to share for future maps.

Also included in this newsletter is a franchise map of Skunkland representing all of the (less than unique) businesses with at least two locations. Franchises are one of the ways that players spread their build vision and participate in each other's builds.


Featured Build: C&C's Bed and Breakfast

A minor wonder in its own right, yrsyrs' franchises of C&C's Bed and Breakfast are spread across central Skunkland. The good name of C&C goes all the way back to before the foundations of the first nations when yrsyrs decided to build and brand quaint shelters in the various villages and along major routes. The C&C Bed and Breakfast locations are easily spotted by their red and yellow roof trim. They always have a safe bed to sleep in and at least one form of food for the hungry traveler. There are now more than a dozen locations including seven sprinkled around Crevatia, Desert Town, Johnville, Sofia, and one in southern Pristina (Gavel). Make sure to note locations on the included Skunkland Franchise Map before planning your next trip over land. C&C Bed and Breakfasts can be located along many major roads in central Skunkland.
