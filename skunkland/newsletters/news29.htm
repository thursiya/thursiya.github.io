Century Build Competition

A big thank you to all who participated in our third Century Build competition; this time we had the most entries yet! The official competition wrapped on May 23, but players are of course welcome to come back and add finishing touches to their builds or add further memories or art pieces in the Expo 400 Gallery, which will be completed in the near future. So if you have a particularly large art piece you wanted to fit in, it's best you do that before the architectural frame is built. Players embraced the 'home' theme, and all six of our participants built a copy of a home they had previously lived in (almost all childhood houses). We also got a handful of fun community memories and a few art pieces for the gallery. The winner of the official competition builds was Shylilie, who participated in every aspect of the competition and presented an incredibly detailed rendition of one of her childhood homes. The winner of the free builds was MCG8, who built numerous homes and won for imagination in her 'Dream Home'. Participants are awarded prize packages including real estate/fair rides in the forthcoming Funland Theme Park, next to Tsai Group headquarters, north of Ghibli Port.

Featured Build: Bank of Gavel Western Headquarters

After a long time deciding what build to add to Delve City, thursiya chose to add a branch of the prolific Bank of Gavel. Being as Delve City is quite significant, this bank was chosen to be not only a regional banking branch but also the western headquarters of the Bank of Gavel, overseeing all Bank of Gavel operations west of Gavel. The bank building is loosely based on the original hunting lodge of Versailles, with the main floor of the north wing housing the Delve City Regional Bank Branch, and the main floor of the south wing housing an investment and loans office (currently the only financial location offering deposits of additional precious materials or construction loans). Inside the main headquarters are various offices, including a governor's suite on the top floor, and a number of storage vaults. The most significant vault is the Main Vault, located on the bottom floor, locked with a redstone password system - the first of its kind. Make sure to check out the governor's suite, gardens and helipad in the back, the map of Bank of Gavel branches, and try to figure out the vault password hidden in a clue somewhere in the building. The Bank of Gavel Western Headquarters can be reached by road in the western suburb of Delve City or soon by helicopter from the Delve Airport.
