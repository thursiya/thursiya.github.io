August 2012

*** The New Kingsburg Monitor ***

Welcome to the new Minecraft Server Newsletter! This is an occasional update to inform players about what's happening on the server and what's changing in the world of Minecraft.

Breaking news! Minecraft version 1.3.1 is now out and this means a number of important changes and updates to you, mainly:
- mobs can now spawn on top of upside down slabs and stairs (regular slab and stair roofs are still safe)
- redstone components and beds can also now be placed on top of upside down slabs and stairs
- trading with villagers (exchange goods for emeralds or vice versa with villagers)
- gain experience from mining ore and smelting
- enchanting level cap of 30 and only 15 bookshelves required for max now
- book and quill item (for writing your own books), and new book recipe (3 paper + 1 leather)
- cocoa beans now spawn and grow on small jungle trees
- trip wires and the ability to place string on the ground
- boats are faster, cut through lily pads, and drop a boat when you destroy them by hand (like a minecart)
- new things to discover (desert villages, temples, emerald ore) if you venture beyond the current world map

For a complete list of changes in this and previous updates see Version History.

Featured Build: Delve

This month's featured build is Delve by Spaceskunks. Delve is the oldest serious build started in Skunkland, under continuous construction from January 2012 to the present. Planned as a large encircling wall with an underground city, the city has gone through a few changes since its foundation, at one time housing a subway terminus (now found on the outskirts), and for a short time maintaining a giant tree in the center of the surface floor. The city is currently about half hollowed out and about a quarter complete. Make sure to check out the giant Delve HQ (Delve.jpg), Apothecary, Portal, Delvion Bank, and the impressive Dragon Entrance. Delve can be reached via subway (west from Desert Town), by heading west up the Spawn River, by road southwest of Desert Town or west through Space Town, or by nether portal.
