*** The New Kingsburg Monitor ***

Saddle up compadres! We are currently on version 1.6.2, the Horse Update. Besides horses and horse paraphernalia there are various items and bug fixes as requested by players. Important new things to be aware of:
- there is a new minecraft launcher required for versions 1.6 on
- horses! (as well as donkeys and mules)
- horse stuff (leads, horse armour, and hay bales)
- carpets
- stained clay blocks
- block of coal (smelts better than 9 coal)
- charcoal now has a different texture than coal
- golden apples are more expensive and less effective
- zombies can now lock on to players from 40m! (previously 16m)

For a complete list of changes in this and previous updates see Version History.

It's been a long time since we've had a newsletter update and that's partly due to builders busy with work and Mojang taking so long to release the new update. Have no fear, there have been great things afoot! Spacenerds and wezzie18 started a new town project west of Delve called Mysteria. Yrsyrs completed the town of Noricum as well as Fort Resolution and is now busy updating the commercial scene in Crevasse. Kokushibyou316 has added a new point of interest by the West Koku station and msteveharrison has embarked on a new project in South Shore inspired by his recent travels in Spain. With the completion of the air traffic control tower and Nethair jet, the Persson International Airport is officially complete and thursiya is finally moving on to new projects. The subway network now extends to the planned Celestia hub station and Thrurail corporation has built the planned connection between Gickerville and Anderson's Crossing - look for massive subway expansion in Crevatia and Gavel over the next 6 months.

The build examples this time stretch from one corner of the known world to the other. Clockwise from top-left: Mysteria (west of Delve); Portal to New Kingsburg (in the nether); Koku's Pacman Treehouse (west Koku Kingdom); Nethair Jet at Persson Airport (west Enlil province, Gavel); Fort Resolution (west-central Crevatian Dominion). In the center: Noricum (west-central Crevatian Dominion, north of Stellarton). We've also included an updated world political map, reflecting shifting borders, new subway, and even the loss of a lake to terraforming.


Featured Build: South Shore

The long delay in newsletters deserves a sizeable featured build, so this month it's the city of South Shore by msteveharrison. Started in September 2012 as a small wharfside settlement with grand ambitions, South Shore has rapidly grown to become one of the largest cities on the server and the capital of the Commune of South Shore. The city's first significant build was the South Shore Inn (currently the largest hotel in the world). Growing out from the eponymous shore, a striking subway station, farming collective, row homes, resort accommodations, international franchises, and capital building have been erected. Currently msteveharrison is constructing a large cathedral on the west side of the city overlooking the river. Make sure to check out the hedge maze, nuclear missile silo, and explore the narrow streets and tunnels criss-crossing the city. South Shore can be reached by subway east from Baths Palace, by road east of Masovia state, or by nether portal.
