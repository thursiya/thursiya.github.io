Featured Build: The Crevasse

This month's featured build is The Crevasse by yrsyrs. The Crevasse started as a large ravine in a swamp, halfway between Delve and New Kingsburg along the newly built subway. Yrsyrs found the location inspiring and decided to construct a ravine sanctuary. During the early days of the Crevasse one could see giant wall murals of Mario characters. The original ravine sanctuary morphed into the large "Home Tree" which would form the center of a sprawling city. The crevasse was hollowed out further in an aggressive TNT restructuring to take on its current form. Much of the swamp on the north side of the city was filled in to make room for a new residential district. The Crevasse is notable for housing the first shops on the server and some of the first foreign franchises. The city is still being expanded, with new stores and houses popping up regularly. Besides the shops in town and the exceptional subway station, make sure to check out some of the interesting things on the outskirts of town, such as the Mechwarrior (which once burned down the top of the Home Tree by accident), Crevatian Palace, Volcano Portal, and the graveyard. The Crevasse can be reached via subway (east from Desert Town), by heading due north from Spawn, by road east of Desert Town or west of New Kingsburg, or by nether portal (in the volcano on the east side of town).
