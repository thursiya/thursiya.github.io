No major changes on Mojang's end since our last newsletter means we are still in version 1.7.2 and rather than trimming to make room for new terrain admin has been busy cleaning up the trimmed terrain around our server (so there are less hideous breaks between old and new terrain). We've also had a number of new builders join us and things have been busier than ever; Christmas Eve we hit a new record: 6 players on simultaneously!

We welcome two new regular builders to our ranks: easy_spirit (who has claimed the land east of South Shore) and nebby28 (who has moved so far south as to not fit on the conventional map). The biggest build news this season would be Msteveharrison & Aninabanina's Moon Base, south of South Shore. Thursiya has consolidated various warehouses into the massive Operis National Distribution Centre. The builders of Mysteria have returned to add a number of new additions. All of this construction has demanded an increase in the subway network. South Shore now has three new connections: east to Porcinia, south to the Moon Base (also now connected to Lunar Hill), and north to the new stop, Mellon Bay. Celestia has added Mellon Bay and Baths Palace connections and the long-awaited connections of Desert Town-William's Harbour and Delve-Mysteria are complete.

Holiday Celebrations

For those players who haven't picked them up already, yrsyrs has left Christmas gifts for all our regulars at Hometree (get off at Crevasse subway station). Also, yrsyrs will again entertain us with a special New Year's Eve fireworks show at 10pm (tomorrow). This year's fireworks venue is the village under Fort Resolution and you can be sure to have an excellent view from the fort itself (get off at Anderson's Crossing subway station then trek northwest along the roads).

Featured Build: Mysteria

Mysteria, founded last year by Spacenerds, Wezzie18, and 123weedie, is the first large-scale cooperative build started on the server. Although initially barely more than a village of slipshod structures it has matured into a fine town of creative new projects. Mysteria was one of the first settlements founded "beyond the trim line". Recently it experienced a revitalization thanks to a new subway line, the demolition of abandoned structures, and the dedication of its founders to completing many small builds. A marketplace is currently under construction and buildings have started spilling out beyond the conventional town boundaries potentially leading to a new city on the server. Make sure to check out the shops, homes built right into the hills, the lovely cottages outside of town, and the epic caverns on the subway route just before you arrive. Mysteria can be reached by subway west of Delve.
