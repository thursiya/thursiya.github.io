Featured Build: The Persson International Airport

The Persson International Airport is a massive project started by thursiya in the summer of 2012. It is located in the southwest corner of the province of Enlil, roughly in the center of Gavel, on the northwest shore of the Sea of Gavel. The airport required months of leveling the local desert terrain (producing the necessary glass for Celestia) and laying out stone for the huge runways. Intended as a reasonable modern metaphor for nether travel, the airport was designed around the idea of having a nether portal in an airplane that could connect to other airplanes in the overworld via dedicated minecart lines in the nether. However, we will have to wait for future airports to be built to see the plan come to fruition. With work done on the terminal, air traffic control tower and the Neth-air jet the airport has officially been completed. Watch for an airport hotel, firestation, and local roadways coming in the future. Make sure to check out the airplane interior and visit the shops in the upstairs of the terminal. Persson Airport can be reached by subway north from Baths Palace or east from Celestia.
