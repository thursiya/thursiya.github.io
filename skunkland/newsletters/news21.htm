Auverland, Faclair - Flooding south of Auverland! University of Ennist scientists cite global warming related to massive industrialization as the likely cause while Gavel scientists dispute the notion that global warming is a human-caused phenomenon stating the mountain is under-utilized as a water resource.

Special Feature: Nether Map

The nether can be a pretty daunting place, especially if you're new to Minecraft, but it doesn't have to be too dangerous. With the help of a map we can assist you in getting around Skunkland faster (as movement in the nether covers 8x the distance it does in the overworld). Mapping the nether is no easy feat in game (you will find you get an ugly fuzz for a map) or out of game as the many overhangs and elevations create obscured maps. This map has been created by compositing maps from various elevations in the nether to give players a good look at portals, roads, and rails - this means the elevation levels change at different points in the map and some features may visually cross but be significantly apart on the Y-axis in game. It should be noted that some sections, such as South Shore, Faclair, and Boatmurdered, are considerably higher than others, and that the Air Travel route is fully enclosed so only accessible from the appropriate overworld portals.


New Server and World Maintenance - The End

With the release of 1.9 the world generation code has changed again. Like 1.8 there has been no change to the basic formation of the world so new chunks will physically look identical to new chunks generated in 1.8 and create no "trimline" gashes in the terrain. Unlike 1.8 there are no new blocks generating in the overworld so there will be no trim of the overworld. Players wanting to avoid trekking to the edges of the known world for some beets can find some planted in the vegetable garden in Desert Town. The End has been updated massively in 1.9 so the current plan is to completely delete The End on Saturday March 12, allowing the new End dimension (and re-summonable Ender Dragon) to appear. Any questions, concerns, or comments should be directed to thursiya.

Now that Spaceskunks has returned "full time" we are ready to move the server to a new address. The move will take place in the following week. The new address will be sent out in an email update.


Featured Build: Cutter's Junction

In late 2013 Msteveharrison started work on a new settlement in South Shore, southeast of the commune's eponymous city. Cutter's Junction was constructed with a distinctively Western style, incorporating a large wood palisade wall, thatch-roofed buildings, and of course a saloon! The town was also the home to one of the very first auto-farms on the server and an early mob grinder design which provided gunpowder for use in the functioning cannon tower. Early experiments with the cannon tower explain the quarry on the north side of town. Construction mostly wrapped two years ago but while Cutter's Junction was being expanded its hub was the Operations building, home to the first "slush pile" hopper-based dump-and-sort and a dedicated minecart materials line. Make sure you check out the sheep ranch, Junction Bakery, stay a night at the saloon, and grab some mob drops from the bottom of the grinder. Cutter's Junction can be reached by road east from South Shore City and west from the Sky Gardens or by nether portal (just east in the contested zone).
