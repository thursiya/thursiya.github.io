February/April 2020

*** First City TV News Network ***

Lunar Hill, Gavel: A small group of what appear to be protesters have gathered outside of the Operis Lunar Hill office. The group is similar to others that have occasionally sprung up across the country avoiding vigilant big city security forces in favour of smaller communities. The latest concern is the shift to factory farming in Operis operations. Reached for comment, an Operis spokesperson responded, "The market has dictated a need for increased production. We continue to focus on meeting shareholder expectations." This wouldn't be the first commercial controversy here as unfounded rumours persist of slave labour bolstering the economy.

Well, thanks to the world being on lockdown, Skunkland has been busier than ever. We continue in version 1.15.2 for the time being, awaiting the next big update that will usher in nether biomes and a number of new blocks.

Since the end of the Century Build contest our players have headed back to familiar territory and begun aggressively tackling long overdue projects. We've also seen the return of SpaceNerds, now with a username shift to Keirclarkson. The economy signs and functions have been completely rewritten to improve legibility, standardization, and ease of price changing. After the Tortilla Flat subway connection was completed new heli-flight destinations from Persson Airport opened to Mellon Bay, Ghibli Port, and Zefram.

Skyscrapers of Skunkland

Our second Skyscrapers of Skunkland contest has come and gone and we had an extra participant this year. Players built embassies in the Gavel capital of Nexus that were in the theme of "Patriotic Pride" (while thursiya constructed the administration centre of the republic: the Nexus Orb). This was the first foray onto the international stage for new state Sedona, an offshoot of Braille. Spaceskunks' intricately-detailed Ennist embassy was declared the winner and it is believed that this year's competition will be held in Alaris.


Century Build

In two weeks time we will officially begin the Year 600 Century Build! Year 600 begins February 26, 2020 and goes until March 2, 2020. This year's theme is "Builders in Motion" and we will be building various contraptions for moving around at the Funland site (abandoned amusement park no more!). Players will add a segment to the Funland Coaster wrapping around the park, create their own ride (that must physically move a player), and have access to an area for constructing their own creative builds. To reach Funland take the road or subway east from the New Kingsburg airport or the road or subway north from Ghibli Port. At the end of the competition prizes will be awarded by Thurail Corporation to all participants and a special prize to the builder of the most original ride. It's time to get loud, it's time to get colourful, it's time to get moving because we're going to the fair!
