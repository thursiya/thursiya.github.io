Important Server World Notice

With the release of the Redstone Update it has been decided to trim the server world again. This trim would involve cutting the overworld down to approximately what is pictured in the last newsletter's political map and removing the nether completely. This will allow new players to gain immediate access to 1.5 terrain by heading south of spawn and will populate the nether with nether quartz. The trim is scheduled for Monday, March 18 so that players have time to recover materials from the nether and outer overworld, and maybe do a last run through before it is refreshed.


Featured Build: The Baths Palace

This time the featured build is the Baths Palace by thursiya. The palace was one of the first significant builds to be completed with the structure, surrounding lake, and canal completed in May 2012. Built by request of thursiya's wife, the Baths Palace is a loose recreation of Łazienki Palace in Warsaw. Having minimal knowledge of the interior of the palace, thursiya recreated a central rotunda and located the eponymous baths in the rear, then built appropriate palatial rooms throughout the remainder. Besides the baths, inside you will find multiple guest rooms, servants' quarters, ballroom, chapel, and a library. The lake around the palace was connected to Moon River and a long canal tunnel was built to connect the back side of the palace to the river dividing South Shore and Masovia state. Make sure to check out the baths, state rooms, and maybe even venture into the hidden tunnels beneath the palace. The Baths Palace can be reached by subway north from Lunar Hill, west from South Shore, or south from Persson Airport. The road connection is still under construction (nearby access is from South Shore or Lunar Hill) and will come as more buildings populate Masovia state and the area around the subway station.
