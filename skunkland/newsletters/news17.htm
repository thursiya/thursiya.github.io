July has been busy with a few old regulars popping in for the summer and our busiest builders hard at work. We are currently running version 1.8.8 (bug update) and rumour has it that version 1.9 is just around the corner. 1.9 will start to roll out features of the "Combat Update" adding things like dual-wielding and enchanted arrows. And if you're looking for new blocks, an organic purple block set is in the works too.

Thursiya is ticking off boxes on a Minecraft to-do list and for July the project was the Gavel Embassy in Crevasse, the first in a series of embassies in major nations. In western Faclair Shylilie has been busy on an aggressive building regimen developing an entire community along the jungle tours route, including a sizeable shopping site elevated in the trees, SkyMart. 123weedie has finally returned to New Kingsburg and has nearly finished a fine home in Northside - a number of lots are still available for players looking to build a home in New Kingsburg. The new Crevasse embassy also features a helicopter nether connection to the air network so travel continues to improve throughout Skunkland.

Featured Build: Auverland

When Shylilie announced a plan to build a large city, something much more ambitious in scale than Cadwgan, and so shortly after joining the server, some players were a little incredulous. Less than a year later we have the city of Auverland, virtually built out, and a top travel destination. Located just west of Endermere (itself only a little west of Cadwgan), Auverland takes up the entire mountainous peninsula in the southwest of Faclair. It is home to Skunkland's second clinic, largest hotel, and first significant public library, playground, and housing developments. For a very short time Auverland was even the largest city in Skunkland and is still one of the largest on the server. Shylilie carefully mapped out the peninsula and planned residential, commercial, and service districts after completing the first large building, the Marchelund Centre. Like many large cities it features a few builds from other players and, as capital of Faclair, has space for foreign embassies to be built. Interesting fact: unlike most players' builds, the large warehouse in the south of the city was actually the last building added to the city. Make sure you check out the wedding services in the Marchelund Centre, nab the honeymoon suite in the Executive Hotel Auverland, find a nice new home from the ample selection, and cap off a run through the hedge maze with a good trampoline jump in the playground! Auverland can be reached by road west of Cadwgan, by subway south from Jungle Tours (west of Cadwgan), or by nether portal.
