*** The New Kingsburg Monitor ***

New Kingsburg, Pristina - "Citizens flock to the Pristina State Courthouse to see its first case of corporate law: Thurail Corporation is suing Thrurail Corporation for trademark infringement. Thurail Corporation, temporarily based out of New Kingsburg until headquarters are completed in Celestia, was founded shortly after the server was started and has worked to link the great population centers of Skunkland. Thrurail Corporation, a Crevatian Dominion state-owned corporation, is the newcomer cutting long distance subway connections across the Crevatian Dominion. The two corporations have competing visions for the global subway network, but so far their feud has not disrupted travel. We can only hope that a settlement will work out in the favour of commuters."

Featured Build: Koku's Fortress

This month's featured build is Koku's Fortress by kokushibyou316. The fortress has been under construction since spring of 2012. Founded in a steep mountain complex south of Gavel, kokushibyou316 quickly built up a massive curtain wall about the entire thing and began clearing out the enclosed bay to make room for a grand tall ship. Key to the fast construction of the fortress was a mob trap built inside the mountain, which allows players to quickly gain the experience needed for enchantments. In recent months the work space on the west side of the fortress has been transformed into the beginnings of a renaissance village. Players are eager to find out what is planned for the flattened cliff tops of the fortress that command an impressive view of the village. Make sure to check out the fortress' entrance, mob trap, village, and of course the tall ship. Koku's Fortress can be reached by road southeast of New Kingsburg or north of Jonville, or by nether portal. Subway access is through the West Koku station (south of Ghibli Port) with direct fortress access coming soon.
