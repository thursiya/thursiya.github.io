Century Build Competition

It's finally here (did the 4th century turn out the way you expected?). Skunk Year 400 begins May 18, 2017 and goes until May 23, 2017 (so coincidentally lining up with Victoria Day long weekend this time around). The century build competition will take place on the Enlil Exposition grounds (take the subway north from Jeda station, in the middle of the province of Enlil, Gavel, near the pyramid) throughout the year 400. If you haven't participated in a Century Build before then you should know that players will have the opportunity to participate in various themed builds and create something of their own choosing at the designated sites in the competition location, using blocks they bring to the site. Some food and beds will be on site available to players. The theme of this competition is "Home away from Home" - the builds interpret how you view Skunkland as a second home. The first build is the literal home: choose a site along the road winding to the northwest and construct a replica of a home you've lived in before in the real world. If you choose to construct an apartment, suite, or other non-detached dwelling then you may want to imagine it as a detached dwelling with roof, etc. The second build is the figurative home: there will be space around the exposition station for two dimensional block artwork or for three dimensional installation works exploring the idea of home. The third build is the home in community: players are asked to share their memories, wishes, thoughts, and important experiences of Skunkland on signs (some provided) on a memory wall. At the end of the competition the signs will be moved to form a special Voices of Skunkland exhibition hall in the dedicated museum facility that will be built up around the second build. Spaces for building your own thing will be available around the central exposition site. At the conclusion of the competition prizes (both blocks/items and intangibles) will be awarded by the Tsai Group to all participants and for the best efforts. We hope to see you in just over a week!
