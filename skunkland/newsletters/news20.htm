Century Build Competition

Thanks to all of the participants who came out during the year 300 to take part in the many elements of our second Century Build Competition. The snowflake will be left unfinished until March in case anyone who missed their chance would like to add blocks. Probably due to the coincidental timing of the Year 300 between Christmas and New Year's, players ended up extending the official theme of "Building Together" into holiday-related builds. 123weedie gave us a large snowman, packed with chests of snowballs for a snowball fight. Shylilie created a gingerbread house and left chests of "candy" blocks for players to decorate it. Spaceskunks was not able to be physically present at the competition so created submissions to be added on return. Thursiya continued the unofficial Christmas theme with "Zefrehem", converting the animal pen into a nativity scene (out of competition due to thursiya representing the host, Doggy Magical Corporation). The winner of the competition was Shylilie, thanks to an impressive basement in the Collaboration House and the Gingerbread House that perfectly captured the theme of encouraging group participation in building. Participants are awarded prize packages including real estate in the forthcoming town of Mellon Bay, next to Doggy Magical's headquarters.

Featured Build: Alaris Farms

At some point in the second century, the people of Skunkland found that mining of metal was not keeping up with demand. Around the same time ocean monuments were added, and Spaceskunks decided it was the right moment to begin construction of various item farms. The Crevasse beacon was temporarily moved to the recently discovered ocean monument off the southwest coast of Alaris (Delve) and work was begun clearing out the monument and preparing it to be a guardian farm. Meanwhile, back on the island province of Alaris, a gold farm (using massive nether portals bringing in wandering zombie pigmen) was started, "The Golden Poppy", and Shylilie joined in to help with construction of an iron farm on the building as well. An iron farm requires iron golems, and they require large numbers of villagers, so the iron farm necessitated construction of a villager farm, this time north of the island across the strait. Today the Golden Poppy draws players from across the globe, who can also stay in the neighbouring "Falling Golem Inn". Make sure you check out the tunnel access to the guardian farm from the sea shack, the hidden opium den under the lake, and Fortenberry Farm to the north. The guardian farm can be reached by nether portal, and The Golden Poppy can be reached by road south from Ennist province, Delve or by nether portal.
