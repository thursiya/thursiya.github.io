World Maintenance

Ever wondered what your friendly server admin is doing in the wee hours of the morning or why sometimes when you're building the darkening world suddenly turns to day? Our server has a strict survival policy, meaning that we pride ourselves in building everything from scratch, collecting the materials and risking certain death as we piece together our world. However, when it comes to server business, thursiya switches hats and breaks into creative mode to deal with whatever it may be. Server business includes checking in with players to make sure everything is working out or to see what they're building for a future newsletter (which often includes teleporting or flying about). It also includes installing economy signs (which require command blocks to place) and making sure the economy system is working correctly. One of the less visible admin activities is world maintenance, which includes repairing environmental damage from endermen and errant creepers, removing floating bits of the environment (such as tree tops without trees underneath or floating islands/blocks created by the world generation code), and smoothing out the terrain. Our world is very old and has been through a few trims which can leave some rough borders between sections of the map (for instance, the massive wall of stone that's been left south of Spawn), so admin attempts to clean up these breaks and make them flow a little more naturally.

Featured Build: World Tree

Although the Jungle Tours project was started a long time ago, it wasn't until quite recently that Shylilie put the finishing touches on its crowning jewel, the massive World Tree that the tour route circles. Like many of the largest builds in Skunkland, this one dogged its creator for a long time and remained partially complete. And just like the surrounding jungle, this tree required a massive amount of imported jungle material (above and beyond what was grown on site). At 91m from entrances to tree tops, it is the tallest "structure" in Faclair. The four main entrances lead into a central domed chamber that looks up into the colossal canopy space, and further staircases lead down into a basement and storage area. Make sure to check out the nearby Jungle Tours site, including a private hot spring and guide shop, and see if you can find the access route to the area above the dome inside the World Tree. The World Tree can be reached by road east of Cirrus or west of Cadwgan, or by nether portal (located right in the tree).
