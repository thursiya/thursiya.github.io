New Contest - Skyscrapers of Skunkland

Shylilie will be moderating a new building contest. Once per real world year, Skunkland players will be able to participate in a 30 day long build contest that is all about making the tallest buildings in Skunkland! The Skyscrapers of Skunkland contest will run every calendar year, starting December 4th and ending in the New Year on January 2nd. Players must bring their own supplies, but a home base with Ender Chest and basic supplies to get each player started will be provided.

Skyscrapers of Skunkland year one will take place next to Auverland, at the west end of the Faclair Airport (which is about to undergo construction so that buildings won't impede the flight paths). Building spots have been set aside, with more spots available at an alternate location if more players are wanting to join in. This year's theme is "Organic Matters", and the contest runs from December 4, 2018 to January 2, 2019. What does Organic Building mean to you? Incorporate that into your style. Skyscrapers must have a minimum of 7 floors and a maximum altitude of Y: 124 (with only small details allowed above that height). They must be office or business use and incorporate one "highest in Skunkland" themed space on the top floor (e.g. highest restaurant, gym, movie theatre). Terraforming (using any "organic" material you like for aesthetics or functionality) from the provided flat ground is very welcome but not required. All participants will receive an award for their builds, and a special prize will be awarded to the builder who best captures the theme "Organic Matters". The grand prize winner will also get to choose the next build site and location! The contest has just begun so grab a site and start building up, up, up...

Century Build Competition

Our fourth Century Build competition may have had smaller scale participation, but it had the largest scale creations yet. Our contestants worked at the special Expo 500 Undersea site, located beneath the waves of the Boreal Ocean, somewhere between the coasts of Crevatia and Gavel (most easily reached through the nether). Players built giant sea creatures outside of the dome and added a deep sea club, sushi restaurant, and pineapple under the sea to the small community in the dome. The winner of the official competition was SpaceSkunks, who constructed an epic bioluminescent octopus threatening to take over the dome. Participants are awarded prize packages, including a special Century Build edition custom gPhone 500 smartphone. Who knows what's in store for our next Century Build?
